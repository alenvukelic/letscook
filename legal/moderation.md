# Moderation Policy

**Last updated:** 2026-05-06 13:22  
**Contact:** moderation@letscook.example

## 1. Purpose and scope
This Moderation Policy describes how letscook handles reports, reviews, enforcement actions, and appeals related to user content and behavior.

## 2. What is moderated
- User-submitted recipes, images, comments, ratings, and profiles.
- Automated signals (spam, abusive patterns) and user reports.

## 3. Reporting content
- Users can report content via the UI with categories (spam, copyright, harassment, explicit content, other).
- Reports should include reason and optional evidence.

## 4. Review workflow
- Reports are triaged by automated filters and human moderators.
- Priority is given to safety and legal violations.
- Moderators may take actions: warn user, hide content (soft remove), remove content, suspend or ban accounts.

## 5. Transparency and notifications
- When action is taken, the uploader receives a notification with the reason and reference to the policy section.
- Moderation logs are kept for audit and appeal.

## 6. Appeals and dispute resolution
- Users may appeal moderation decisions via a defined process (submit appeal form to moderation@letscook.example).
- Appeals are reviewed by a different moderator or escalation team.
- Final decisions and rationale are communicated to the appellant.

## 7. Automated enforcement and rate limits
- New accounts may be subject to stricter rate limits and automated checks to prevent spam and abuse.
- Repeated violations escalate to stronger enforcement.

## 8. Content retention and evidence
- Removed content may be retained in logs for a limited period for investigation and legal compliance.
- Moderation actions and audit trails are stored securely.

## 9. Moderator conduct and governance
- Moderators follow a documented code of conduct and conflict-of-interest rules.
- A transparency log of policy changes and major enforcement trends should be maintained.

## 10. Emergency takedowns
- For content that poses immediate harm or legal risk, emergency takedown procedures apply and may bypass normal timelines.

## 11. Contact and reporting
- For moderation inquiries: **moderation@letscook.example**
- For legal takedown notices (copyright, privacy): follow the process described in Image Rights Policy and Privacy Policy.
