## 1. Purpose
This Privacy Policy explains what personal data **letscook** collects, why it is collected, how it is used, how long it is retained, and the rights users have regarding their personal data. This document is a living stub intended for editing and expansion to meet legal and regulatory requirements (including GDPR).

## 3. Categories of data collected
- **Account data:** email, display name, password hash, OAuth provider identifiers.
- **Profile data:** optional profile picture (uploaded), bio, preferences, language.
- **Content data:** recipes, comments, ratings, complexity votes, favorites, uploaded images and media.
- **Usage data:** IP address, device and browser metadata, timestamps, view counts, search queries, and logs for security and analytics.
- **Communications:** messages sent to support, verification emails, and transactional emails.

## 4. Purposes and legal bases
- **Service provision:** to operate the platform and provide features (contractual/legitimate interest).
- **Authentication and account management:** email verification, password reset (contractual).
- **Content hosting and display:** store and serve user-submitted recipes and media (contractual/consent for media).
- **Security and abuse prevention:** fraud detection, rate limiting, moderation (legitimate interest).
- **Analytics and improvement:** aggregated, anonymized metrics to improve the service (legitimate interest).
- **Legal compliance:** respond to lawful requests and enforce Terms of Use (legal obligation).

## 5. Cookies and tracking
We use cookies and similar technologies for session management, security, and analytics. Users should be informed and given choices where required by law.

## 6. Data sharing and third parties
- **Service providers:** hosting, email delivery, image processing, analytics — only as necessary and under contract.
- **No automatic external image embedding:** external images are not fetched or embedded automatically.
- **Legal requests:** personal data may be disclosed to comply with legal obligations.

## 7. Data retention
- Account and content data are retained while the account is active.
- Deleted accounts: personal data removal or anonymization will follow documented retention schedules; some audit logs or aggregated metrics may be retained for legitimate purposes.
- Media retention: uploaded media are retained according to the media retention policy; removal requests are processed per the moderation and takedown policy.

## 8. User rights (examples; implement per jurisdiction)
- **Access:** request a copy of personal data.
- **Rectification:** correct inaccurate data.
- **Erasure:** request deletion of personal data (subject to retention rules and restrictions for content with interactions).
- **Portability:** request export of personal data in a common format.
- **Restriction/Objection:** restrict or object to certain processing.
- **Withdraw consent:** where processing is based on consent.

Requests should be sent to **privacy@letscook.example** and will be handled within the legally required timeframe.

## 9. Special notes about user content
- Recipes and media uploaded by users are treated under the Image Rights Policy and Terms of Use. Users grant the platform the license necessary to display and distribute content as described in Terms of Use.

## 10. Security measures
We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures (encryption in transit, secure password hashing, access controls, server-side sanitization of HTML content, media validation and scanning). This is a living list and must be kept up to date.

## 11. Minors
Users under the applicable age threshold must not register without parental consent. The platform will remove accounts that are found to belong to minors in violation of the rules.

## 12. Changes to this policy
This document may be updated. The current version and last updated date will be published in the repository and linked from the application footer.
