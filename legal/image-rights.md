# Image Rights Policy

**Last updated:** 2026-05-06 13:27
**Contact:** images@letscook.example

## 1. Purpose
This policy explains rights, responsibilities, and rules for images and other media uploaded to letscook.

## 2. Ownership and representations
- Uploaders represent and warrant they own or have the necessary rights and permissions to upload and license the image.
- Uploading an image does not transfer ownership to letscook.

## 3. License granted to letscook
By uploading media, the user grants letscook a worldwide, non-exclusive, royalty-free license to:
- Store, display, reproduce, and serve the media within the service.
- Generate thumbnails, resized versions, and derivatives necessary for display and performance.
- Use the media in connection with the service (including search results, parent/child recipe displays, and public pages).

## 4. Prohibited media
- Images that infringe third-party copyrights or trademarks.
- Images that violate privacy or depict minors in sexual or exploitative contexts.
- Images containing malware or disguised executable content.

## 5. External images and embedding
- **External image embedding is prohibited.** All images must be uploaded to the platform. External sources may be cited in text but must not be embedded or auto-fetched.
- If a user references an external source, they must ensure they have the right to reproduce the content and must upload a copy if required.

## 6. Attribution and credits
- If an image requires attribution under its license, the uploader must provide appropriate credit in the recipe metadata or description.
- The platform will display attribution where provided.

## 7. Takedown and dispute process
- Rights holders may submit a takedown request with proof of ownership.
- The platform will follow a documented takedown workflow and provide notice to the uploader.
- Uploaders may submit counter-notices per the Moderation Policy and applicable law.

## 8. Retention and removal
- Media associated with content that is removed or accounts that are deleted will be handled per the retention schedule and Privacy Policy.
- Some copies (backups, audit logs) may be retained for a limited period for legal or operational reasons.

## 9. Security and processing
- Uploaded media are validated for MIME type and scanned for malware.
- Filenames are normalized and files are stored outside the webroot.

## 10. Contact
For image rights inquiries and takedown notices: **images@letscook.example**
